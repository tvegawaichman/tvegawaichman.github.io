About

I am a computational biologist interested in understanding cell identity across normal development and diseased using single-cell and spatial transcriptomics. My research focuses on reference mapping, cell annotation, cross-condition comparisons, and the development of reproducible computational tools for genomics.

Research

Cell Identity Mapping

Developing methods for robust transfer of cellular identities across references, species, and disease conditions.

Spatial Transcriptomics

Characterizing tissue organization and disease-associated cellular niches through spatially resolved genomics.

Gene Regulatory Networks

Studying transcriptional programs driving cellular states and lineage transitions.

Software

CoRAL

Quantification of lineage programs at single cell resolution
